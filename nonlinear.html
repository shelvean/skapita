Damped Pendulum

\begin{cases} \dot{x} = y \\ \dot{y} = -0.1 y - \sin(x) \end{cases}
Domain: [-7, 7] × [-5, 5]

Van der Pol Oscillator

\begin{cases} \dot{x} = y \\ \dot{y} = 1 \cdot (1 - x^2) y - x \end{cases}
Domain: [-3, 3] × [-3, 3]

Lotka-Volterra

\begin{cases} \dot{x} = 1 \cdot x - 0.1 \cdot x y \\ \dot{y} = 0.075 \cdot x y - 1.5 \cdot y \end{cases}
Domain: [0, 50] × [0, 30]

Duffing Oscillator

\begin{cases} \dot{x} = y \\ \dot{y} = x - x^3 - 0.15 y \end{cases}
Domain: [-2, 2] × [-2, 2]

Simple Nonlinear

\begin{cases} \dot{x} = x - x^3 \\ \dot{y} = -y \end{cases}
Domain: [-2, 2] × [-2, 2]

Limit Cycle

\begin{cases} \dot{x} = -y + x(1 - x^2 - y^2) \\ \dot{y} = x + y(1 - x^2 - y^2) \end{cases}
Domain: [-2, 2] × [-2, 2]
