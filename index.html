Research interests and background

My research focuses on numerical analysis and scientific computation. I am particularly interested in the design and application of numerical methods to partial differential equations that arise in science and engineering. Recently I have been thinking about Bernstein-Bézier and multivariate spline methods for solving variational inequalities, particularly the elliptic obstacle problem. Special interests include numerical PDE constrained optimization, numerical methods for wave propagation, artificial boundary conditions for unbounded domain problems, and discontinuous Galerkin methods.
